Duration and buffer

Duration is the appointment itself. Buffer is extra time held after it — cleaning down, notes, turnaround. The next booking cannot start inside the buffer, so a 30-minute service with a 10-minute buffer occupies 40 minutes of the day.

One site or all of them

Leave “Offered at” blank and every location can book this service. Pick a location and only that site offers it. Most businesses leave it blank for their core services and pin only the specialised ones.

What the agent says

The description is read aloud when a caller asks what a service involves. Write it the way you would say it on the phone, not the way you would print it on a price list.
