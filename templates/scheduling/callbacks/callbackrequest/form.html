Not knowing who rang is normal

Leave the contact blank when you do not know who called — that is the ordinary outcome of an inbound call, not a gap to fill in. Whatever name or number you did catch is enough to work the callback, and it can be attached to a contact later.

The number to ring back on

This is often not the number they rang from — "call my mobile instead". It is kept exactly as you type it, so an extension or an “ask for Dana” note survives; a value that is nothing but a number is tidied into international form.
